The Classroom (Start Here)

  • The classroom is where you should get started. It'll give you a brief overview of pretty important topics, such as technical analysis, trading strategies, risk management, and more.
  • The classroom also contains a TON of vocab. Don't feel obligated to memorize all of them. Instead use it as a reference if you ever have a question on a certain vocab term
    • On the top right you should see a "Search" bar. If you ever have a vocab question or even a general question, this will make it extremely easy to find anything you need.
